Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work, or to work more efficiently, and to provide information to the site owner.

2. How We Use Cookies

L2D Driving School uses only essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the website to function. We do not use any advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies.

3. Essential Cookies

The following cookies are set by this website:

Cookie Purpose Duration
sessionid Keeps you logged in as you navigate between pages. Set by Django. Session (deleted when you close your browser)
csrftoken Protects against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks when you submit forms. Set by Django. 1 year

These cookies are essential for the secure operation of the site. The website cannot function correctly without them.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some features on this website involve third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Maps — An embedded map is displayed on the Contact page. Google may set cookies when you interact with the map. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — The contact form submits data to Formspree. Formspree may set cookies as part of its service. These are governed by Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Calendly — Approved students can book lessons via an embedded Calendly scheduler. Calendly may set cookies. These are governed by Calendly's Privacy Policy.

We have no control over third-party cookies. You can block them via your browser settings without affecting your use of the core site features.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Deleting or blocking the sessionid cookie will log you out of the site. Instructions for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Continued use of the site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
